Phase Awareness

Track Phase 1 (optimal) vs Phase 2 (degrading). Know when Claude is at peak performance.

Alert Levels

Info 40% Approaching mid-context, still in Phase 1
Warning 50% Optimal checkpoint point, consider saving
Critical 70% Entering Phase 2, degradation may begin
Emergency 85% Serious degradation, save immediately
Capacity 95% Context nearly full, auto-compact imminent
